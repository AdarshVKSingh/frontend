Spectra 90


NextGen Hybrid VTOL Drone For Ensuring Cost Effective maintenance of Energy Infrastructure!!

Spectra 90 is a Hybrid VTOL Drone enabled with AI based technology to offer a valuable tool for energy infrastructure inspection, providing enhanced safety, efficiency, and data collection capabilities, and helping to improve the overall reliability and safety of global energy infrastructure as well as for large scale mapping.

Specifications of Spectra 90



  • Versatile Operations: No need for a runway or launchpad.
  • Flight Endurance: 90 Min.
  • Payload Capacity: 2 kg
  • Wing Span: 2000 mm



The demand for VTOL drones continues to grow as industries recognize their unique capabilities and the potential they hold for improving operational efficiency, safety, and data collection. With advancements in technology and increased awareness of their benefits, VTOL drones are becoming an integral part of various sectors, contributing to enhanced productivity and innovations.



Applications of Spectra 90

Aerial Mapping & Surveying

Infrastructure Inspection

Emergency Response and Search and Rescue

Environmental Monitoring

Delivery and Logistics

Precision Agriculture
